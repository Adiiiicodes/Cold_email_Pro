Guidelines

Follow these steps to create the perfect cold email

Step 1: Prepare Your Portfolio

  • Convert your portfolio to PDF format
  • Include detailed project descriptions
  • Highlight key achievements and metrics
  • Ensure file size is under 10MB

Step 2: Answer Questions

  • Be specific about your experience
  • Use quantifiable achievements
  • Include relevant certifications
  • Focus on recent projects

Step 3: Job Requirements

  • Provide complete job posting URL
  • Ensure page is publicly accessible
  • Check for detailed requirements
  • Verify company information

Step 4: Review and Send

  • Review generated email thoroughly
  • Customize as needed
  • Check for accuracy
  • Save for future reference
